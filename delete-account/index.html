Delete Account

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

FishBite Tech ("we", "us", or "our") provides users with the ability to request deletion of their account and associated data.

You can request account deletion at any time by following the methods below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: In-App Deletion
You can delete your account directly within the app by navigating to:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

Option 2: Request via Email
If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us:

Email: kraken@fishbite.xyz

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your account email address
  • Your user ID (if available)

Data Deletion Details

When your account deletion request is processed:

  • Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 7 days
  • Subscription and transaction records may be retained as required for legal and financial compliance
  • Some anonymized data may be retained for analytics and service improvement

Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription, please note:

  • Subscriptions are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • You must cancel your subscription through the respective platform before deleting your account
  • Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions
